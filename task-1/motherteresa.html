Mother Teresa

Mother Teresa dedicated her entire life to serving the poor, sick, and dying. Through her Missionaries of Charity, she touched millions of lives around the world with compassion, love, and tireless effort.

Her message of peace and service remains powerfull: "Not all of us can do greate things.But we can do small things with great love." She remains a global symbol of charity and humanity.
